How to restore dented bumper?
There are a few ways to fix a dented bumper, depending on the severity of the dent and your DIY skills. Here are some options:
* **For minor dents:** You might be able to use a plunger or boiling water to gently pop the dent out from the inside. Be sure to research the specific technique for your car model to avoid causing further damage.
* **For moderate dents:** A professional paintless dent repair (PDR) technician can often fix the dent without needing to repaint the bumper. This is a good option if you want a professional finish and don't mind spending a bit more money.
* **For major dents or damaged bumpers:** You may need to replace the bumper entirely. This is the most expensive option, but it's the best way to ensure a like-new appearance.
